GlassBank

A modern mobile banking experience designed for clarity and trust

UX/UI Design Mobile App Figma
Hero Mockup

Overview

The Problem

Traditional banking apps often overwhelm users with complex interfaces, hidden fees, and confusing navigation. Users struggle to complete simple tasks like sending money or checking their balance quickly.

The Solution

GlassBank provides a transparent, intuitive mobile banking experience with a clean interface that puts essential actions front and center, making everyday banking effortless.

My Role

Lead UX/UI Designer responsible for user research, wireframing, visual design, prototyping, and usability testing.

Timeline

4 weeks from research to high-fidelity prototype

Design Process

01

Research

Conducted competitive analysis of existing banking apps and interviewed users to understand pain points with their current banking experience.

02

Ideation

Created user personas, journey maps, and low-fidelity wireframes to explore different approaches to solving the identified problems.

03

Visual Design

Developed a clean, modern design system with a focus on accessibility and visual hierarchy that builds trust through transparency.

04

Prototyping

Built interactive prototypes in Figma and conducted usability testing to validate design decisions and iterate based on feedback.

Learnings & Outcomes

What I Learned

  • Trust is earned through transparency in financial apps
  • Progressive disclosure helps reduce cognitive load
  • Clear visual hierarchy is essential for quick actions
  • Consistent feedback builds user confidence
